About Club America

The one place where everyone matters, the one place you can actually be free. Our vast student-made group takes pride in educating and guiding students during their time in Van High School. Through cheap pancakes in the morning, and free milkshakes on float day on Fridays. Here at Club America, we’re more than a group, we’re a community where every student belongs, and every voice is heard.

Mission

Encourage civic involvement and educate students on economics and civics through respectful debate and community events.
